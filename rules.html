Turn Phases

  1. Draw: Pull one card from your deck at the start of your turn.
  2. Attach: You may attach exactly one Energy card to one of your Pokémon.
  3. Play: Put Basic Pokémon onto your Bench, play Trainer cards, or Evolve.
  4. Attack: Use one attack from your Active Pokémon, then your turn ends.

How to Win

In the Pokémon TCG, there are three primary ways to claim victory in the arena:

  • 1. Take All Prize Cards: Knock out your opponent's Pokémon to collect all 6 of your Prize cards.
  • 2. Knock Out the Active: Win if your opponent has no Pokémon left on their Bench to replace a Knocked Out Active Pokémon.
  • 3. Deck Out: Win if your opponent has no cards left in their deck at the start of their turn.
